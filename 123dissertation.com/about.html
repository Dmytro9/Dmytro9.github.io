Getting started is as easy as 1-2-3 thus the name of the website. Just make sure you have the paper instructions by your side before proceeding.

Step1

Your first step will be creating an account on the website by clicking Submit Details or Order Now button on any page of the website;

Step2

Your second step will be to fill in the order form providing vivid and detailed paper instructions – you're also able to upload all of the necessary files and reading materials at this point;

Step3
